Как говорить время

o'clock — ровно
quarter past — 15 минут после
half past — половина (30 минут)
quarter to — без 15 минут
ten past, twenty to, five past и т.д.

O'CLOCK PAST HALF TO
It's three o'clock.

🐟 1. Соедини часы и время

Нажми на часы, потом на фразу (или наоборот). Правильные пары загорятся зелёным.

😸 2. Напиши время словами

Пример: 1:30 → It's half past one.

b) 5:30 It's half past five.
c) 11:00 It's eleven o'clock.
d) 3:15 It's a quarter past three.
e) 6:45 It's a quarter to seven.
f) 12:10 It's ten past twelve.
g) 8:20 It's twenty past eight.
h) 1:50 It's ten to two.
i) 4:35 It's twenty-five to five.
j) 11:25 It's twenty-five past eleven.
k) 2:55 It's five to three.

📖 6. Чтение: A day with Ginger Cat

🐱 Ginger is a funny orange cat. He wakes up at seven o'clock but stays in bed until quarter past seven. Then he has a quick shower and eats breakfast at half past seven — fish, of course! He catches the bus (a real cat on a bus? 😺) at eight o'clock. He goes to cat school at quarter past eight. He plays with a ball of wool at ten past ten. Lunch is at twelve o'clock. After a long nap, he does homework at four o'clock. Dinner is at half past six. He watches TV at eight o'clock and goes to bed at quarter to ten. Purrfect day!
